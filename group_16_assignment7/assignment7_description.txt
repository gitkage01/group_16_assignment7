Rules of play:
1. Enemies fall at a given rate from top of screen
2. Player can move horizontally at the bottam of the screen
3. Player can shoot enemies, making them disappear
4. Enemies will keep spawning until the game is over

Win/Lose conditions:
1. Win: no win condition. Just try to get the highest score
2. Lose: lose if any of the enemies reach the bottam of the screen

Classes:
There are three classes. The "Player" class is dedicated for the controllable player.
The "Enemy" class is dedicated for the enemies that fall from the top of the screen.
The "Button" class is dedicated for the interactables in the menus.
